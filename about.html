Mampallil School of Hospitality and Tourism (MSHT)

MSHT is formed to upgrade skills of youth and tackle manpower needs of hospitality and tourism industry. MSHT is mandated to create a robust and sustainable eco-system for skill development in hotel industry. MSHT aims to cater the need for industry-ready workforce in all sub sectors, namely, Hotels, Tour Operators, Food Service Restaurants, Facilities Management and Cruise Liners.

Our training courses are organised in association with industry partners through work integrated learning programmes and on-job training with stipend.
