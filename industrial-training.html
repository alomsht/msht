Certificate in Hotel Management

The tourism and hospitality industry has emerged as one of the key industries driving growth of the services sector in India. The hotel industry promises a bright future, and our Certificate programme in Hotel Management prepares candidates to build lucritive career in this sector.

This is a 3 months Work Integrated Learning Programme. It is an on-job training, and does not include classroom session. The course is taught through on-job industrial training in reputed hotels with stipend. We accept applications from candidates having any bachelors degree or diploma. The industrial training and intsernships will provide ample opportunities to students to identify prospective employers and find jobs in reputed hotels. We also provide strong placement support.
